The Buns!

A little game made with p5 by Ziping Chen

"The Buns" is inspired by T-Rex Chrome Dino Game. In this game, you will take control of a small steamed bun trying to escape from a bustling bun shop. However, be cautious of hungry customers, as touching them will bring an end to your journey.

Winning Condition:

  1. Control the bun to reach the far right of the screen, avoiding collisions with customers, and escape from the bun shop.
  2. Control the bun avoid collisions with customers and challenge the longest survival time.

Use the following keys:

  • WASD keys are used to control the bun
  • Press "R" to reset the game.
